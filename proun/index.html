Proun is a random art generator, designed to give inspiration to visual artists.

The control panel allows you to adjust the shape, size, and colour of dynamically generated art.

Press Space, or click on the button at the bottom of the screen to change the random seed, and generate a new image, with either the same settings, or random settings.

You can save what you generate by copying the url, which contains all the information to generate the art again.
